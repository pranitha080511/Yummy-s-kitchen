Steps to place an order

  • Log in with your existing account or create a new one using your email or phone number.
  • Explore a wide variety of dishes and cuisines. Use categories or search to find your favorite food.
  • Tap on the dish you want, customize if needed (e.g., spice level, quantity), and click “Add to Cart.”
  • Once done, go to your cart to review your selected items, apply available offers or promo codes.
  • Enter or confirm your delivery address and preferred delivery time
  • Pick a payment option - UPI, card, wallet, or cash on delivery.
  • Hit the “Place Order” button to confirm.
  • Use the real-time tracking feature to see the order status and estimated delivery time.
  • Receive your food hot and fresh at your doorstep. Don't forget to rate and review!
